SOCIAL MEDIA PROMOTION PLAN

Client: GlowWell Skincare

Note:
"GlowWell Skincare" is a fictional business name used for the purpose of this academic project.
All branding, promotional material, and digital content were created solely for learning and demonstration.

What product/service is being promoted?
GlowWell Skincare offers natural, eco-friendly skincare products including cleansers, moisturizers, serums, and sunscreen. The brand focuses on clean beauty with ingredients that are gentle on all skin types, cruelty-free, and sustainably packaged.

Who is the target audience?
Young adults aged 18-35, primarily women (but inclusive of all genders), who are health-conscious, environmentally aware, and interested in clean beauty. This also includes skincare beginners looking for simple, effective routines.

Which platforms are most suitable?
Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube are ideal for skincare tutorials, before-and-after results, product demos, and influencer collaborations. Instagram and TikTok are visual-first platforms that align well with beauty content, while YouTube supports longer-form educational videos like skincare routine guides.

Proposed Hashtags:
#GlowWithGlowWell
#CleanBeautyEssentials
#NaturalSkincare
#GlowWellRoutine
#EcoFriendlyBeauty

Promotional Strategy:
GlowWell Skincare will leverage Instagram Reels and TikTok for short, engaging skincare routine videos and product demos, while using YouTube for in-depth tutorials and ingredient breakdowns. We will partner with micro-influencers in the clean beauty space to build authenticity and trust, and run seasonal campaigns (e.g., "Summer Glow Challenge") to encourage user-generated content and grow community engagement organically.
